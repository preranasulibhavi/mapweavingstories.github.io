Art in Action

Sculpting Dreams and Building Teams at Chitra Santhe, Dec-Jan(2025)

At the heart of this year’s Chitra Santhe lies a project that beautifully embodies the spirit of art and collaboration. A sculpting endeavor, ambitious in both size and symbolism, is underway: a 9-by-9-foot hand crafted from thermocol, spread like a flower in full bloom. This awe-inspiring installation is not just a testament to artistic vision but also a reflection of the unity and dedication of the team bringing it to life.

Sculpting the Hand of Expression

Jan, 2025

The centerpiece, a giant hand spreading like a flower, is designed to captivate and inspire. Its material, thermocol, is both challenging and versatile, demanding precision and creativity from the artists involved. The hand symbolizes openness, creativity, and the beauty of shared expression. As it takes shape, the sculpture invites viewers to ponder the strength and grace that art can embody, even when built from the simplest of materials.

Chitrasanthe

The theme of the 22nd edition of Chitra Santhe in Bengaluru, India, which will be held on January 5, 2025, is "Protecting Women: A Shared Responsibility"
